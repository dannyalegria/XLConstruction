Welcome to the XL Construction Safety Training Portal where we practice thinking safe, working safe, and going home safely every day.



XL Construction’s commitment to safety will ensure that the project is completed without incident, and that all workers, owners, and the public go home safely at the end of each day.

To continue and further our efforts toward providing the safest possible construction site, XL has teamed up with ClickSafety to provide “on-line” training for Subcontractor Safety as well as other programs.

The Subcontractor Safety Program (SSP) training is required for all subcontractor personnel who will perform work on XL Construction projects. While the information contained in the SSP was created to apply on all XL projects, please remember that there are unique requirements for certain job sites. In these cases, you will be given a list of site-specific requirements when you arrive on the project.

For XL Construction, there is simply nothing more important than the safety of everyone involved in or affected by our construction projects.
